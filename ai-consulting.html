FAQ

Common questions

What does an AI consulting engagement produce?

A prioritized, specific answer to where AI fits in your operation — what to do first, what it takes, and what to ignore. If building it makes sense, we can do that too; if it doesn’t, we’ll tell you.

We’re a small business. Is AI consulting worth it for us?

Often yes, precisely because small teams feel repetitive work the most. The honest test is whether a specific workflow is eating hours every week — if nothing does, you probably don’t need us yet.

Will you just tell us to buy AI tools?

No. Tools are the easy part and rarely the problem. The value is in knowing which of your workflows are worth changing and how to wire them so the change sticks.

What makes your advice credible?

We run a multi-product software studio on the same playbook — our own operation is coordinated by AI agents, living documents, connected tools and scheduled automations. We recommend what we actually run.
