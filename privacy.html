Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

This policy explains what information the Casting Karma LLC website (castingkarmallc.com) collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. What we collect

We keep data collection to what the site actually needs:

  • What you send us. When you use the discovery tool or contact us, we collect the details you provide — your name, the way you’d like to be reached (email or phone), and what you tell us about your needs.
  • Analytics. We use privacy-respecting analytics (Google Analytics) to understand aggregate, anonymized traffic — pages visited and general location — to improve the site.
  • Technical basics. Standard server and security logs (such as IP address and browser type) handled by our hosting and network providers.

2. How we use it

To reply to you and discuss working together, to operate and improve the website, and to keep it secure. That’s it.

We do not sell your information, share it with third parties for their marketing, or use it to train AI models.

3. Who processes it

We use a small set of trusted providers to run the site: our hosting and network provider (Cloudflare), our analytics provider (Google), and our email delivery provider (Resend) which routes discovery and contact messages to our inbox. They process data only to provide those services.

4. Retention &amp; your choices

We keep the messages you send us for as long as needed to follow up and maintain our records, and no longer than necessary. You can ask us to access or delete the information we hold about you at any time by emailing contact@castingkarmallc.com, and we’ll take care of it.

5. Cookies

The site uses only the cookies needed to run it and to measure aggregate analytics. You can block or clear cookies in your browser without losing the core experience.

6. Children

This is a business website and is not directed at children under 16.

7. Changes

We’ll post any material changes here and update the effective date above.

This policy covers the castingkarmallc.com website. Individual software products we operate may have their own product-specific terms and privacy policies. Questions? Email contact@castingkarmallc.com.
