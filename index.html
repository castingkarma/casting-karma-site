FAQ

Common questions

What does "done-with-you AI implementation" actually mean?

It means we build the thing, in your tools, alongside your team — rather than handing over a strategy document and leaving. You end up with a working system and the understanding to run it yourself.

What kinds of work can you automate?

The repetitive, rules-shaped work that quietly eats hours: client follow-ups, recurring reporting, data entry between apps, reminders, routing and triage, summarizing inbound messages or documents, and keeping your numbers in one place.

Do we need to know where AI fits before we start?

No — most teams don't. The first step is mapping how your operation runs today, and that's usually what surfaces the highest-leverage place to start. If a workflow isn't worth automating, we'll tell you that too.

How are engagements structured?

Fixed-scope and framed around a specific outcome — not open-ended retainers. Tell us the workflow, and we'll tell you what it takes.

What tools do you work with?

The common business stack — email, CRM, spreadsheets, cloud storage, billing — plus most apps with an API. If a tool you rely on can't be connected, we'll say so up front rather than promise around it.

Where are you based, and do you work remotely?

Casting Karma LLC is based in Texas and works with clients across the United States, remotely.
